The training dataset is sourced from IQVIA Inc., which is de-identified for public access. The whole training dataset contains 1713 samples, where 855 samples are diagnosed as wild-type amyloidogenic TTR cardiomyopathy (ATTR-CM) (case group) and 858 samples (sample per row). The training dataset is separated into two parties. Party 1 (party_1.csv) contains 831 samples (500 cases vs 331 controls). Party 2 (party_2.csv) contains 882 samples (355 cases vs 527 controls). Each sample has 1874 phenotypes (features per column).
